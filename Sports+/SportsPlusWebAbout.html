About Sports+

To provide you with a brief background on our small business, SPORTS+ is a new apparel store that caters to an exclusive market. Our focus is to provide up market apparel and accessories and position ourselves as the top retail store servicing this particular market. We are seeking to expand into the wider Waikato with the establishment of three SPORTS+ branches.
